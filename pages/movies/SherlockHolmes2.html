Sherlock Holmes 2 a Game of Shadows

Intregue, mistery, chess and a race against time, all while atempting to beat a master chess player in it's own game, this is a very complex and enjoyable movie for any lover of a good detective work

The themes, places, fights both mental and phisical are amaizing and out of the ordinary for an explosion and stunt loving audience, it feels fresh in many ways.
